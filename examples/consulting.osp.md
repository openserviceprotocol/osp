# StrategyWorks AG

> Management consulting for mid-market companies (50-500M revenue) 
> in DACH. Specializing in market entry strategy, operational 
> transformation, and M&A due diligence. Engagement types range 
> from focused 2-week sprints to multi-month transformation programs.

## Available Services

- [Market Entry Strategy](/osp/services/market-entry.yaml): Market analysis, partner evaluation, go-to-market planning for new geographies or segments
- [Operational Assessment](/osp/services/ops-assessment.yaml): 2-week diagnostic of operational efficiency with quantified improvement potential
- [M&A Commercial Due Diligence](/osp/services/cdd.yaml): Independent assessment of target's market position, customer base, and growth potential
- [Transformation Program](/osp/services/transformation.yaml): Multi-month implementation of strategic and operational changes with measurable KPIs

## Not Available

- IT system implementation (we advise, we don't build)
- Recruitment / executive search
- Legal or tax advisory (we partner with specialized firms)
- Companies below CHF 20M revenue

## Conditions

- Minimum engagement: CHF 25,000
- Regions: Switzerland, Germany, Austria (with European project scope)
- Languages: de, en, fr
- Proposal response time: < 5 business days
- All engagements are consultative — scope is developed in dialogue
