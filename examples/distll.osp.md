# distll

> We make human-delivered services machine-readable, orderable, and 
> deliverable — for the era of AI agents. Advisory, tools, and 
> methodology based on the "Service as a Software" approach. 
> Industry-agnostic, for DACH companies with 50+ employees.

## Available Services

- [Service Cartography & Assessment](/osp/services/assessment.yaml): Map and prioritize all services by agent-relevance, automation potential, and volume. Deliverable: prioritized service map and transformation roadmap.
- [Service Decomposition & Skill Formalization](/osp/services/skill-formalization.yaml): Break down services into atomic skills using adaptive expert interviews, automated extraction from existing catalogs (ServiceNow, SAP), and digital artifact analysis. Deliverable: machine-readable skill documents in OSP format.
- [Orchestration Design](/osp/services/orchestration-design.yaml): Model how skills work together — workflows, decision points, human-in-the-loop, error handling. Deliverable: implementable orchestration models.
- [Pilot Implementation](/osp/services/pilot.yaml): Build technical infrastructure and launch the first 3-5 skills at maturity level 1 (Human Skill) to level 2 (Assisted Skill). Deliverable: live API facade with real operations.
- [Scaling & Maturity Coaching](/osp/services/scaling.yaml): Ongoing support for horizontal expansion (more skills) and vertical development (higher maturity levels). Deliverable: growing skill catalog with decreasing token costs per service.
- [OSP Implementation](/osp/services/osp-implementation.yaml): Create osp.md, service manifests, and MCP integration to make services discoverable and orderable via the Open Service Protocol. Deliverable: validated, token-efficient OSP files ready for publication.

- Process consulting without agent perspective (classical BPM)
- Companies under 50 employees (effort-to-value ratio not justified)
