# TransLogistics AG

> International transport and logistics for temperature-controlled 
> and conventional goods across Europe. B2B, from single pallets 
> to full truckloads. GDP-certified for pharmaceutical logistics.

## Available Services

- [LTL Transport](/osp/services/ltl-transport.yaml): Partial loads, 5-15 pallets, temperature-controlled available, 48-96h across Europe
- [FTL Transport](/osp/services/ftl-transport.yaml): Dedicated truck, all temperature zones, 24-48h
- [Parcel & Pallet](/osp/services/parcel.yaml): Single pallets and parcels, European network, 24-72h
- [Customs Clearance](/osp/services/customs.yaml): CH-EU import/export, certificates of origin, preference calculation
- [Warehousing](/osp/services/warehousing.yaml): Storage, picking, returns, temperature-controlled facilities in Basel and Rotterdam

## Not Available

- Air freight
- Ocean freight
- Dangerous goods classes 1, 6, 7
- Destinations outside Europe, UK, Norway

## Conditions

- Minimum order: CHF 150
- Regions: Switzerland, EU, UK, Norway
- Languages: de, fr, en
- Quote response time: < 2h during business hours (CET)
- Insurance available up to CHF 500,000 per shipment
